Campaign Framework

Based on Eugene Schwartz's "Breakthrough Advertising" (1966) — applied to modern intent data and multi-channel B2B campaigns.

The Model

5 Levels of Customer Awareness

1 Unaware — Doesn't know they have a problem
2 Problem Aware — Knows the problem, searching for answers
3 Solution Aware — Knows solutions exist, researching categories
4 Product Aware — Evaluating specific vendors and products
5 Most Aware — Ready to buy, needs the right offer

Applied Example

The Coherent Example

One company's communication problem manifests differently at every level. All Problem Aware — same problem, different words.

End User "how to explain technical problems to non-technical people"
Manager "how to understand development teams"
HR "anonymous employee feedback tools"
CEO "reducing SaaS company turnover rates"

Four different campaigns needed. Same problem. Same company. Different words, different roles, different entry points.
