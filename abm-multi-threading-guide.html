The Framework

Three Roles in Every B2B Deal

From Apollo.io's "Outbound Sales, No Fluff" + MEDDIC methodology. Every target account has three distinct personas you need to reach.

🎯

The Buyer

Who

Signs the check. CXO, VP, Director with budget authority.

Cares About

ROI, risk reduction, competitive advantage, board-level metrics

Keywords They Search
[solution] ROI reduce [problem] costs [competitor] pricing best [category] for [industry]
Copy Approach

Numbers, case studies, competitive benchmarking

"Companies using intent data reduce CAC by 34% in Q1. See the math."
🏆

The Champion

Who

Internal advocate sounding the alarm. Usually mid-senior management.

Cares About

Solving the problem their team faces, looking good to leadership, making the right recommendation

Keywords They Search
[solution category] comparison how to fix [specific problem] tools for [specific workflow]
Copy Approach

Problem validation, solution frameworks, easy-to-share resources

"Your team's communication problem isn't unique. Here's how 200+ SaaS companies fixed it."
👤

The End User

Who

Actually experiences the problem daily. Engineers, sales reps, marketers.

Cares About

Making their daily work easier, being heard, reducing frustration

Keywords They Search
how to [solve daily friction] why does [tool] suck [specific task] best practices
Copy Approach

Empathy, quick wins, relatable pain points

"Tired of explaining the same technical concept 5 different ways? There's a better way."
The Evidence

Why Multi-Thread?

Apollo.io's research found targeting ALL THREE roles simultaneously shortens B2B sales cycles significantly.

Slow
Buyer Alone

Long cycle, may not see the urgency. No internal pressure to act.

Stuck
Champion Alone

Can't sign the check, may lose internal momentum over time.

Weak
End User Alone

No authority, can only create bottom-up pressure. Easily ignored.

Wins Deals
All Three Together

Urgency from below, advocacy from middle, decision power from top.

Getting Started

Quick Start: Single Avatar

Not every client needs full ABM. Start simple and layer complexity as engagement data comes in.

Pick the Champion

Usually the most responsive persona. They are actively searching for solutions and willing to engage with your content.

Run Problem Aware + Solution Aware Campaigns

Two awareness stages give you top-of-funnel education and mid-funnel positioning without overwhelming your budget.

Layer in Buyer Targeting

Once champion engagement is confirmed through clicks, downloads, or replies, start running ROI-focused ads to the decision maker.

Add End User Targeting

Create bottom-up pressure. When the end user is asking for your solution internally, the buyer hears it from every direction.
